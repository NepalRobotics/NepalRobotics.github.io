About Us

We are a consortium of students, academics, and industry leaders working largely on a voluntary basis to develop Unmanned Aerial Systems as tools for the public good.

Our Goals

This project runs along two tracks: first, we wish to experiment with UAS as a platform to aid in search and rescue efforts and, second, we wish to increase engagement with and collaboration about UAS in institutes of learning around the world. To these ends, students at the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute are developing an open source project to create autonomous vehicles capable of aiding rescuers in the search for buried avalanche victims.
